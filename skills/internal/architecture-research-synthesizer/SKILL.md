# Architecture Research Synthesizer

## Workflow

1. Read the idea brief and any cited inspirations.
2. Extract useful mechanisms, training lessons, and open questions.
3. Rewrite them as:
   - reusable constraints
   - recombination opportunities
   - explicit anti-copy warnings

## Required output

- `research synthesis`
- `do-not-copy notes`
- `design opportunities`

## Guardrails

- Summarize inspirations at mechanism level, not implementation-template level.
